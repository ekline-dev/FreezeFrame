# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 2026

FreezeFrame is built on the principle of absolute privacy. 

## Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** * **No Telemetry:** The application does not phone home, track usage statistics, or log your hardware configurations.
* **No Screen Recording Storage:** The screen mirroring process happens entirely in your computer's temporary graphics memory (RAM/VRAM). FreezeFrame does not save, record, or upload any images or video of your screens.
* **Offline Capable:** The core functionality of FreezeFrame requires no internet connection to operate.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding privacy or security, please refer to our `SECURITY.md` file to contact the development team.
