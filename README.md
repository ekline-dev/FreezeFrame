## ✨ About The Project
Whether you need to freeze a presentation slide while multitasking in the background, anchor a dashboard, or secure quick privacy, FreezeFrame has you covered. It runs quietly in your system tray with zero lag—holding your screen perfectly still until you choose to thaw it.

## 🚀 Features
* **True Zero-Lag:** Lightweight architecture ensures your system resources stay free.
* **Seamless Background Multitasking:** Work on other monitors or in the background while the main display stays locked on your chosen frame.
* **Discreet Operation:** Runs silently in the background without intrusive pop-ups.
